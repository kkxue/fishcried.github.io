> 这篇是我自己所了解的关于Dragonflow Pike版本的动态，仅供参考。

### Pike版本实现的特性
- IPv6

- Trunk ports (VLAN aware VMs)

- SFC

- Service Health Report

- BGP

- Distributed SNAT


### Queens版本计划实现的特性
- Kuryr Integration

- RPM and DEB Packaging

- LBaaS (Dragonflow native and distributed)

- FWaaS

- TAPaaS

- L3 Flavour

- Troubleshooting Tools

- Multicast and IGMP
